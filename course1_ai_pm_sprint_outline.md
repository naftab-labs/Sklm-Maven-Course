# COURSE OUTLINE #1: AI PM Sprint
## Title: "AI Product Management Sprint: Ship Your First AI Feature"
## Format: Workshop + 2-Week Mini Cohort Hybrid

---

## WORKSHOP: 1-Day Intensive (4 Hours)
### Saturday 10am-2pm PST

### Hour 1: AI Mental Models for PMs (10:00-11:00am)
**Goal:** Shift from "AI is magic" to "AI is a tool I can ship"

**Content:**
- 10:00-10:15 | **Welcome + Intros** - Who's here, what they want to ship
- 10:15-10:35 | **The PM's AI Stack** - LLMs, embeddings, agents explained in PM terms
- 10:35-10:55 | **AI Feature Taxonomy** - Classification vs generation vs recommendation vs agent
- 10:55-11:00 | **Break**

**Activities:**
- Map your current product: Where could AI fit? (worksheet)
- Group brainstorm: "What AI features have you seen recently that actually work?"

**Deliverable:** Each student identifies 2 potential AI features for their product

---

### Hour 2: From Idea to AI PRD (11:00am-12:00pm)
**Goal:** Write a spec for an AI feature that engineering can build

**Content:**
- 11:00-11:20 | **The AI PRD Template** - Prompts, evals, edge cases, fallback
- 11:20-11:40 | **Prompt Engineering for PMs** - Zero-shot vs few-shot vs chain-of-thought
- 11:40-12:00 | **Defining "Good Enough"** - Setting quality bars and eval metrics

**Activities:**
- Write a prompt for your AI feature (live in ChatGPT/Claude)
- Peer review: "Would you ship this?"

**Deliverable:** V1 PRD for one AI feature

---

### Hour 3: Prototyping Without Engineers (12:00-1:00pm)
**Goal:** Build working demos to test with users before coding

**Content:**
- 12:00-12:10 | **Break + Lunch**
- 12:10-12:30 | **No-Code AI Stack** - Replicate, Bubble, Vercel v0, Claude Artifacts
- 12:30-12:50 | **Live Build** - Everyone prototypes their AI feature in 15 min
- 12:50-1:00 | **User Testing 101** - How to test AI features without confusing users

**Activities:**
- Build rough prototype (students share screens)
- Test with 1 other student and get feedback

**Deliverable:** Working prototype + feedback notes

---

### Hour 4: Shipping Strategy + Next Steps (1:00-2:00pm)
**Goal:** Create a 30-day plan to actually ship this feature

**Content:**
- 1:00-1:15 | **Stakeholder Management** - Getting buy-in from skeptical eng/sales/exec
- 1:15-1:30 | **Risk Mitigation** - Hallucinations, bias, latency, costs - how to handle each
- 1:30-1:45 | **30-Day Ship Plan** - Week-by-week breakdown from prototype to production
- 1:45-2:00 | **Cohort Pitch** - What's next in the 2-week intensive (upsell to $497)

**Activities:**
- Write your 30-day plan (worksheet)
- Group share: "I'm shipping X by [date]"

**Final Deliverable:** 30-Day Ship Plan + Prototype + PRD

**Post-Workshop:**
- Email with resources (prompt library, PRD template, no-code tools)
- Invitation to 2-week cohort ($497) - **TARGET: 4 upsells**

## MINI COHORT: 2-Week Intensive (4 Sessions)
### Tuesday/Thursday 6:00-7:30pm PST

### Session 1: Validating Your AI Feature (Week 1, Tuesday)
**Goal:** Make sure you're building the RIGHT thing before building it

**Agenda (90 min):**
- 6:00-6:15 | **Check-in** - What did you ship this week? Blockers?
- 6:15-6:35 | **User Research for AI** - Jobs-to-be-done framework + AI-specific discovery questions
- 6:35-6:55 | **Problem-Solution Fit** - Is AI actually the right solve? Framework for validation
- 6:55-7:15 | **Live Work** - Everyone runs 2-3 user interviews (using worksheet)
- 7:15-7:30 | **Share + Feedback** - What did you learn? Pivot or persevere?

**Pre-work:** Run 3 user interviews before class
**Homework:** Refine PRD based on user feedback
**Deliverable:** Validated problem + updated PRD

---

### Session 2: Building the V1 (Week 1, Thursday)
**Goal:** Actually build your feature (with support)

**Agenda (90 min):**
- 6:00-6:15 | **Check-in** - What changed after user interviews?
- 6:15-6:40 | **Architecture Decisions** - Build vs buy vs partner, latency vs quality tradeoffs
- 6:40-7:00 | **Prompt Engineering Deep Dive** - System prompts, context windows, token optimization
- 7:00-7:30 | **Coworking Block** - Everyone works on their feature in real-time + office hours

**Pre-work:** Bring your PRD and any code/builder work so far
**Homework:** Build V1 of your AI feature (no-code or low-code OK)
**Deliverable:** Working V1 ready for testing

---

### Session 3: Testing + Iteration (Week 2, Tuesday)
**Goal:** Make your AI feature actually good enough to ship

**Agenda (90 min):**
- 6:00-6:15 | **Check-in** - Demo your V1 (2 min each)
- 6:15-6:40 | **AI Evals for PMs** - How to measure quality, spot drift, set thresholds
- 6:40-7:00 | **Edge Cases + Failures** - When AI fails gracefully, when to escalate to humans
- 7:00-7:30 | **Iteration Workshop** - Fix your worst failure mode live

**Pre-work:** Run evals on your V1, find 3 failure cases
**Homework:** Fix critical issues, prep for user test  
**Deliverable:** V2 of feature + eval results

---

### Session 4: Ship Week Strategy (Week 2, Thursday)
**Goal:** Launch into production or internal beta

**Agenda (90 min):**
- 6:00-6:15 | **Check-in** - What's your ship confidence 1-10?
- 6:15-6:40 | **Beta Launch Playbook** - Rollouts, monitoring, rollback plans
- 6:40-7:00 | **The Ship Decision** - Framework for "is this good enough?"
- 7:00-7:20 | **Demo Day** - Everyone ships or presents launch plan
- 7:20-7:30 | **Graduation** - Certificates, alumni Slack, next cohort preview

**Pre-work:** Prepare 5-min demo of your shipped (or soon-to-ship) feature
**Deliverable:** Shipped feature OR 7-day launch plan

**Post-Cohort:**
- Private alumni community access
- Monthly office hours (ongoing)
- Next cohort early bird ($100 off)
