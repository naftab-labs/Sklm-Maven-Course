Questions? We've Got Answers

Do I need to know how to code?
Nope. This course is designed for PMs, not engineers. We'll use no-code and low-code tools so you can build prototypes without writing code. If you want to go deeper later, you can.
What if I've never worked with AI before?
Perfect. This course assumes zero AI background. We'll start with the mental models and build up. By the end, you'll have shipped something—most courses just teach you about AI.
Can I get my company to pay for this?
Absolutely. Most students expense this as professional development. We can provide an invoice and course description for your manager.
What if I can't make the live sessions?
Recordings are available for 30 days, but we strongly recommend attending live. The value is in the real-time feedback and accountability.
What's the difference between the workshop and cohort?
The workshop is a one-day intensive where you learn the foundations and build a prototype. The cohort is two weeks of guided implementation where you actually ship your AI feature with feedback and accountability. Many students do both.
